Programming shine tsari na ƙirƙirar jerin umarni waɗanda ke gaya wa kwamfuta yadda ake yin wani aiki. Ana iya yin shirye-shirye ta amfani da yarukan shirye-shiryen kwamfuta iri-iri, kamar JavaScript, Python, da C++. Shirye-shiryen umarnin yana taka muhimmiyar rawa a zamanin na'ura mai kwakwalwa na yau saboda yana ba da damar ƙirƙirar software na computer, aikace-aikace, gidajen yanar gizo, da ƙari mai yawa. Akwai harsunan shirye-shirye da yawa da ake samu, kowanne an tsara su tare da takamaiman dalilai da lokuta masu amfani. Python website shine shafin rasuwar kayan kamfanoni na Python. W3Schools shine shafi na HTML. W3Schools CSS shine shafi na CSS. W3Schools JavaScript shine shafi na JavaScript.

Binciken bayanai shine wani abu mai amfani da za a iya yin cikas da kwarewa ga bayanan sabbin al'umma. A Najeriya, binciken bayanai ke yin amfani dashi ga wadanda suka tafi kwana da al'umma tsakanin ababen gina halin da suke cikin. Wannan abu ke yin amfani ga damuwa da bayanan sabbin tsarin takardar, siyasa, dala, da sauran bayanan, domin ganin yadda al'umma suka fara iya amfani dashi.

1. Data Camp (A nan na samu babban kursin binciken bayanai da Python. Zaka iya yin amfani da Python don yin bayanan sabbin al'umma a kan bayanan da suka gabata.)
2. Matplotlib (Haka ne, shafin rubutu na Matplotlib. Zaka iya samun bayanan sabbin masu amfani ga data visualization a kan data a matsayin bidiyo da kayan kamfanoni.)
3. Pandas (Pandas shine sassanci masu zuwa ga aikin bayanai na data analysis da data manipulation a Python. A nan za a samu bayanan da suke amfani dashi.)
4. Scikit-Learn (Scikit-Learn shine kayan kamfanoni masu zuwa ga aikin bayanai na machine learning a Python. Zaka iya samun bayanan sabbin masu amfani domin yin amfani da su a matsayin sabbin masu amfani a kan binciken bayanai.)
5. R Project (R Project shine wani kayan kamfanoni don bayanai a cikin takardar da siyasa. A nan za a samu bayanan masu amfani domin ganin cikin bayanan siyasa a cikin data analysis.)
6. Tableau (Tableau shine kayan kamfanoni don bayanai da data visualization. Zaka iya samun bayanan da suke amfani dashi domin tsarin data da data visualization.)
7. Apache Hadoop (Apache Hadoop shine kayan kamfanoni masu zuwa ga aikin bayanai na big data. A nan za a samu bayanan masu amfani domin tsarin big data analysis.)

Menene Kwamfuta?

Kwamfuta na’ura ce mai aiki da ƙwaƙwalwa, wajen karɓa da sarrafawa da adanawa da kuma miƙa bayanai, a sigogi daban-daban. Wannan shine ta’arifin kwamfuta a takaice. Bayan haka, kwamfuta na tattare ne da manyan ɓangarori guda biyu; ɓangaren gangan jiki, wanda aka fi sani da Hardware, a turance. Sai kuma ɓangaren ruhi ko rai, wanda ake kira Software (manhaja), shima a turance. Kafin mu yi nisa, mai karatu zai ji ana fassara kalmomin turancin nan, saɓanin yadda zai gamsu ko yake ganinsu a cikin ƙamus (Dictionary). Hakan ya faru ne saboda a yanzu muna cikin wani zamani ne mai suna “turban masana”, ko Information Highway, kamar yadda bayani ya gabata a ƙasidar Matambayi ba ya ɓata. A wannan zamani, kalmomin harsunan duniya za su yi ta sauyawa ne iya gwargwadon fannin ilimi ko rayuwar da ake amfani da su. Don haka sai a kiyaye
